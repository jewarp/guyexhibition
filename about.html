My practice focuses on methods of photomontage. Google Satellite has been my choice of resource since 2020, enabling me to have a birds eye view of regions and locations beyond my everyday reach. Taking elements that I discover on my travels I combine them to create sculptural forms in two dimensions. Using the technology of my day I create new patterns and structures that are informed and influenced by the quirks of that technology.

- Guy Gibson

Born, lives and works in London
